Onyee's Recommendations

My favorite Ghibli movie out of all of the movies that Miyazaki has created is Princess Monoke. It's a movie that speaks on the relationship that we as humans have with the enviroment.

With any of the Ghibli movies I definitely recommend watching them subbed as opposed to dubbed. Some of the translations and acting in the dubbed version isn't bad but I always watched these movies subbed so it holds a sentimental meaning watching it in it's original form 😎

The one Ghibli movie I would caution any audience watching is Grave of the FireFlies. While the movie is good (it feels weird to say that considering it's subject matter) it is a tough watch as it goes through the story of 2 children in war torn Japan and their struggle to survive on their own. While the movie is hard to get through, I do think there is something about covering deeper topics of life like this in an animated film...but watch at your own risk.
